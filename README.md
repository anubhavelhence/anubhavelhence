## 👤 About Me

Hi there, my name is Anubhav Elhence

I’m a Ph.D. candidate at BITS Pilani, where I work under the ASEAN India Collaborative Project funded by SERB DST. My research focuses on enabling drone-driven smart cities through real-time computer vision, secure communication, and data fusion for drones. I also serve as the Lab In-charge of the IoT Tinker Lab, mentoring over 20 students and managing an 80 lakhs INR funding. My academic journey integrates fields like signal processing, machine learning, computer vision, embedded systems, and software development, enabling me to innovate at the intersection of hardware and software.

I have gained experience across various sectors, including IT, R&D, telecommunications, and defense industries. In these roles, I've contributed to significant projects in intelligent systems, robotics, generative AI, image processing, and digital communications. These experiences demonstrate my ability to navigate complex challenges and drive interdisciplinary projects in both academia and industry. With a passion for technology and a versatile skill set spanning multiple domains, I'm well-positioned to make lasting contributions through innovation and teamwork.

Check out my portfolio, CV, and GitHub résumé to learn more about me and my projects.
